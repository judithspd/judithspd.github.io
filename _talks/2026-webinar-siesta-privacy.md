Event: EOSC SIESTA webinar: data privacy tools and trusted data repositories.

Speaker and organizer of the event.

Date and place: March 18, 2026. Online.

Talk: Data anonymization in practice: anjana and pyCANON

Summary: In this talk, we will provide an introduction to data anonymization techniques and showcase the use of two Python libraries: Anjana and pyCANON. Anjana can be used for data anonymization, and pyCANON allow to check the level of anonymization of a tabular dataset. In addition to demonstrating these libraries, we will present their use through two interactive interfaces deployed in EOSC SIESTA, which enables a simple and user-friendly process.
